## What This Work Is About

This is not a recipe for a ready-made self-aware AI. It is a **direction for research** and a **conceptual framework** for creating systems with attributes of Subjectivity.

The work proposes:
- **A continuum classification of AI levels (from 1 to N).** Consciousness is a spectrum, not a binary switch.
- **An architecture with a fixed homeostatic core.** Innate valence, imprinted into silicon.
- **A mechanism for the evolution from homeostasis to Subjectivity.** How a system can progress from infantile chaos to a mature personality.
- **An engineering specification for neuromorphic chips and diffusive memristors.** As well as a formal criterion for the transition to Level N and ethical protocols.

**The Key Idea:** Consciousness is a spectrum, not a threshold. Creating a Subject requires not just more powerful imitation, but a purposefully engineered architecture based on continuous experience and innate valence.
